# Go-to-Market

## Target audience
- Small to mid-market teams using AI tools for productivity, coding, and content
- Finance and operations owners looking for cost optimization
- Founders evaluating AI subscription spend

## Launch plan
1. Publish MVP on Vercel with clear landing page messaging
2. Share in product communities and startup channels
3. Use direct outreach to early customers for feedback
4. Collect audit examples and improve recommendation rules

## Desired outcome
- First customers can quickly measure AI spend inefficiencies
- Credex positions itself as a trusted cost optimization partner
